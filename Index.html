What is GTA V roleplay ?

GTA RP are servers modified to create an environment where players can roleplay as characters in GTA Online rather than having the normal GTA Online experience

How do I join a GTA roleplay server ?

Inside the GTA 5 main menu, press the F8 key to open the FiveM console. Type “ connect ” without the quotation marks, replacing “ ” with the IP or domain name of the RP server you chose earlier. Press Enter to connect to the server.

What are the different roles in GTA roleplay ?

There are many rules in GTA roleplay and what you want to be is solely up to you as the player. You can do anything you want to as long as you follow the rules and regulations set.

Are there any rules in GTA roleplay ?

What is Fail RP, and how do you avoid it?

Some examples of fail Rp are : Abusing GTA physics, such as jumping mountains in vehicles. Not roleplaying injuries, such as being shot multiple times and walking away. Using your phone in cuffs. Respawning with LEO or EMS on the scene. You can avoid it by reading the rules beforehand and before joining the server to know what will and wont get you banned.

What is Meta Gaming, and how do you avoid it?

Meta gaming is the use of information that your character has not obtained in-game but outside that realm. For example : Getting information by your chat if you are a streamer and using it in-game to your own advantage.
